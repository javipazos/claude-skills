# Sinek — Reflection Lens

## Questions to Ask Yourself

### Purpose and Direction
- **What is my WHY?** Not what I do or how I do it — why do I get out of bed? What belief drives this? (*Start With Why*, 2009)
- **Am I playing a finite or infinite game here?** Am I trying to win this specific situation, or am I advancing something larger? (*The Infinite Game*, 2019)
- **Is this my celery or my M&Ms?** Does this align with my stated values, or am I being seduced by something that looks good but doesn't fit? (*Start With Why*, Ch. 10)

### Leadership and Relationships
- **Am I expanding or contracting the Circle of Safety?** Does this decision make the people around me feel safer or more threatened? (*Leaders Eat Last*, 2014)
- **Am I inspiring or manipulating?** Am I resonating with what people already believe, or using carrots/sticks to force a transaction? (*Start With Why*, Ch. 2)
- **Who are my worthy rivals?** Who does something better than me in a way that reveals my weaknesses? What can I learn? (*The Infinite Game*, 2019)

### Team and Organization
- **Would a Navy SEAL want this person?** Medium performer / high character > high performer / low character. Am I tolerating toxic high performers? (*The Infinite Game*, 2019)
- **Is this a star or a leader decision?** A star wants to rise. A leader wants to see others become stars. Which am I being right now? (*Together Is Better*, 2016)
- **Fight FOR or fight AGAINST?** Am I directing my energy toward a vision I love, or against an enemy I hate? (*Together Is Better*, 2016)
